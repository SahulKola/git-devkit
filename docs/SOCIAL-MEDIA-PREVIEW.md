### 1. Timing
- Share during peak hours (9 AM - 6 PM in your timezone)
- Post on days with highest engagement (Tuesday-Thursday)
- Repost popular content every 2-4 weeks

### 2. Hashtags
```
#git #github #productivity #developer #devtools 
#gitworkflow #gitcommands #softwaredevelopment 
#programming #coding #devkit #opensource
```
